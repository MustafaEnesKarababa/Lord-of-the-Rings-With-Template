Lord of The Rings

Information about the lore of The Lord of the Rings, staying faithful to J.R.R. Tolkien's works.

why eagles dont carry the ring ?

Eagles don't carry the Ring in The Lord of the Rings because it is believed that they could be corrupted by its power. Additionally, Sauron's watchfulness would have made it difficult for the eagles to reach Mount Doom without being intercepted. From a storytelling perspective, the journey of the Ring allows for character development and narrative tension.

Who is Gandalf ?

Gandalf is a wise and powerful wizard, known as Gandalf the Grey or Gandalf the White after his resurrection. Gandalf plays a crucial role in the events of Middle-earth, guiding and aiding the protagonists in their quests to defeat the forces of evil. He is characterized by his long beard, staff, and iconic gray or white robes. Gandalf is revered for his wisdom, knowledge of ancient lore, and his commitment to protecting the free peoples of Middle-earth.

Nenya, is it powerfull ?

Nenya, also known as the Ring of Adamant or the Ring of Water, is one of the created by the elven-smith Celebrimbor in the Second Age and given to Galadriel, the Lady of Lothlórien. Its power is centered around preservation, protection, and concealment. It grants Galadriel the ability to ward off evil, maintain the beauty and enchantment of Lothlórien, and shield the realm from the influence of Sauron.

Who is Tom Bombadil ?

Tom Bombadil is a mysterious and enigmatic figure who resides in the Old Forest near the Shire. Tom Bombadil is described as a stout, merry fellow with a blue coat, yellow boots, and a wide-brimmed hat.Tom Bombadil possesses a unique and unaffected nature, seemingly unaffected by the power of the One Ring. He has control over the creatures and spirits of his realm, including the Old Forest and the Barrow-downs. He sings and speaks in rhythmic verses, displaying a deep connection with nature.
